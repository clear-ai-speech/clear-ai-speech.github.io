ClearAI: AI-Driven Speech Enhancement for Patients with Hypophonia

Hypophonia is a common speech symptom related to Parkinsonâ€™s disease, affecting human comprehension and effective communication. Specifically, hypophonic speech is characterized by its low volume and breathy voice quality, which makes it challenging to be heard and understood by both people and voice-control systems, especially in noisy environments.
Conventional speech enhancement techniques, primarily focusing on amplifying audio power or canceling environmental noise, fall short in improving the intelligibility and perception for hypophonic speech. To fill the gap of enhancing hypophonic speech, we present ClearAI, an innovative AI-powered technique to improve speech quality for individuals suffering from hypophonia. ClearAI first leverages voice conversion technology to create a parallel dataset composed of healthy and corresponding hypophonic speech samples. Moreover, ClearAI incorporates a predictive model trained on augmented parallel data to estimate the optimal audio style from hypophonic speech, to strengthen the audio intensity and enhance the speech patterns.
